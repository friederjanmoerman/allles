Moxa, what?

Moxa, or Mugwort, is the herb Artemisia Vulgaris, which has been used for centuries in Traditional Chinese Medicine. Known for its warming properties, Moxa stimulates the body's energy flow, or Qi, through heat.

Moxa season!

The transition from autumn to winter is a sacred time in Chinese Medicine, as during this period the "life gate" (Ming Men) is open, allowing us to strengthen our body and immune system.

According to the energetic seasonal calendar, this transition occurs in November. During this sensitive time, when our "life gate" is open, we are more vulnerable to illness, inflammation, and cold, which is why it coincides with the peak of cold season and flu. To protect ourselves from illness and cold, it is important that our body retains the warmth of summer throughout the winter. Moxa is an excellent way to add extra warmth to the body, giving our immune system an additional boost.
