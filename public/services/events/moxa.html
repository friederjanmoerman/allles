Moxa Ceremony

Moxa, what?

Moxa (or Mugwort) is no ordinary herb. It’s Artemisia Vulgaris, a mighty plant that’s been used for centuries in Traditional Chinese Medicine. Known for its warming powers, Moxa stimulates your body’s energy flow (or Qi) with heat—perfect for winter!

It’s Moxa season!

In Chinese Medicine, the shift from autumn to winter is a sacred time. This is when your "life gate" (Ming Men) is wide open, making it the perfect moment to strengthen your body and immune system. Think of it as your internal "immune boost button"—and it’s activated in November. During this season, your body is more vulnerable to illness, colds, and inflammation, so we need to keep that summer warmth locked in all winter long.

That’s where Moxa comes in—because we could all use a little extra warmth, right? It’s like giving your immune system a cozy blanket.

What to expect?

  • A little Goodie Bag (Yes, please!)
  • Silent Disco (Who knew wellness could be this fun?)
  • A guided meditation (Chill out, zen mode on)
  • Total Relaxation (Your body and mind will thank you)
  • Immunity Boost! (Because who doesn’t need that?)

During the session, we'll use Moxa Heat Packs on specific points of your body to bring that warming magic. We’ll kick things off with a quick intro about Moxa’s origin and benefits, followed by a guided meditation. The goal? Get your "life gate" nice and toasty and ready for winter—consider it a natural flu shot, minus the needles.

The best part? Your Moxa packs will stay warm for at least 11 hours, so you’ll keep benefiting from the treatment even after you leave. It’s like a Moxa-powered, immune-boosting hug that lasts.
