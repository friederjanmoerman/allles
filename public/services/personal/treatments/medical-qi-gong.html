Qi Gong

Medical Qi Gong is a TCM practice that combines movement, breathwork, posture, and visualization to balance energy and improve health. By using specific exercises and focused breathing, it helps unblock energy pathways, reduce stress, improve circulation, and strengthen the immune system.

This practice is effective for boosting vitality, relieving pain, supporting recovery from illness, and enhancing mental clarity.

Medical Qi Gong offers a natural, holistic approach to restoring balance and improving overall wellbeing.
