About the creator behind it all

Meet Leslie Page. Once a biomedical student dissecting the intricacies of the human body, she traded lab coats for sketchbooks and walked out of university with a Fine Arts degree—and a mind too curious to settle for the conventional.

The next chapter? A whirlwind of movement, travel, and curiosity. From diving into yoga and Ayurvedic practices to earning certifications in Personal Training and Sports Massage, the journey eventually led to immersing in the ancient wisdom of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Since then, TCM has become a lifelong pursuit, constantly deepening and evolving. Call it curiosity, call it passion. Call it the art of wonder and balance.

Somewhere along the way, working as a barista revealed a different layer of nourishment—one that went beyond the cup. It was a reminder that health isn’t just about what we put in our bodies, but how we experience the world through taste, smell, and connection. A growing interest in nutrition soon blended with the deeper understanding of TCM: health is about savoring life in all its flavors.

The philosophy is simple: health isn’t just the absence of illness; it’s a celebration of cycles, movement, and connection. Blending ancient wisdom with real-world practicality, the approach is playful, down-to-earth, and refreshingly free of incense clouds. allles (logo) won’t promise instant enlightenment, but Leslie might just teach you to breathe better, move smarter, and laugh at life’s absurdity—all while weaving in techniques that honor the rhythms of nature, body, and mind.

No gimmicks, no fluff—just a clear invitation to find balance, live with ease, and embrace the beauty of life’s flow.
